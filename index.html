Why We Exist

Possibility shouldn't depend on proximity.

Our Story →

Young people of color in Richmond deserve more than what's been put in front of them. We're here to change what they see — and what they believe about themselves and their future.

We don't exist to save anyone. We exist to expose, empower, equip, and encourage — because there is room for young people of color everywhere: in boardrooms, creative industries, skilled trades, entrepreneurship, and every space they choose to occupy.

Access
Dismantling the obstacles that keep young people from the opportunities they deserve to enter.
Exposure
Introducing youth to careers, industries, and environments they may not otherwise encounter.
Empowerment
Developing confidence, clarity, and the conviction to pursue what's possible.
Possibility
Reinforcing that their dreams are valid, attainable, and absolutely worth pursuing.
