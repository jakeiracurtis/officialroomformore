Why It Matters

Every dollar creates more access.

Room For More runs on community support. Every contribution — no matter the size — directly funds the programs, experiences, and resources that help young people of color in Richmond see, believe, and become more.

We create exposure experiences, workshops, career panels, and mentorship opportunities that expand what young people believe is possible for their futures.
